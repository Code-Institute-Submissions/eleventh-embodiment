Few words about me

My name is Ana but my Kundalini name is Harpreet Seva Kaur. I began my yoga journey 6 yeas ago and have been blessed with many inspiring teachers to support me on my path. I have been practicing Kundalini yoga for the last 6 years and have supplemented my practice with Hatha Raja, Tantra, Bhakti, Yin and other styles.

For the past few years I have been traveling and teaching in Bali, Thailand and mostly in Rishikesh, India, where I am based for the last 2 years. I am very passionate about stress and anxiety relief through yoga and different healing arts. Through my inward journey towards healing old traumas, I began to experiment with, and facilitate, different healing arts (Reiki, Shamanic Breathwork and Sound Healing)...
