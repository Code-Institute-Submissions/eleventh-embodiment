My Story

Most people know me as Ana but as a Kundalini teacher I am proud to carry the name Harpreet Seva Kaur.

I began my yoga journey 6 yeas ago and have been blessed with many inspiring teachers to support me on my path. I have been practicing Kundalini yoga for the last 6 years and have supplemented my practice with Hatha Raja, Tantra, Bhakti, Yin and other styles.

For the past few years I have been traveling and teaching in Bali, Thailand and mostly in Rishikesh, India, where I am based for over 2 years now.

I am very passionate about stress and anxiety relief through yoga and different healing arts.

Through my inward journey towards healing old traumas, I began to experiment with, and facilitate, different healing arts (Reiki, Shamanic Breathwork and Sound Healing)...

Methods I use in my practice

Kundalini Yoga

The ancient yoga practice focuses on breath and asana (postures) along with chanting, meditation, and singing. Practice to increase your self-awareness by silencing your mind and unblocking your chakras so that your vital energy can flow freely. Some of the most popular health benefits of Kundalini include a faster metabolism, better mood, and lowered stress levels.

Yin Yoga

Yin Yoga is a slow-paced style of yoga as exercise, incorporating principles of traditional Chinese medicine, with asanas (postures) that are held for longer periods of time than in other styles.
